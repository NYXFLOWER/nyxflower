### `> about_me`

CS PhD student at **UC San Diego**, advised by Prof. Nuno Bandeira. Developing trustworthy machine learning and computational methods for biomedical science, with a focus on proteomics, graph neural networks, and mass spectral library search.

Previously researcher at **Queen's University** Computational Systems Biology Lab (Prof. Laurence Yang) and ML consultant at **Samumed LLC**. Honoured MSc in CS from the **University of Sheffield** — Fretwell-Downing Prize for Best Dissertation. BSc from **Central South University**.
